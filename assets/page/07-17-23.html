Outsmarting Scammers with AI: A Father's Tale of Revenge

Project information

  • Category: AI
  • Project date: 13 July, 2023

In a world plagued by incessant robocalls and scams, one father took matters into his own hands after an incident involving his son. Meet Roger Anderson, the mastermind behind a revolutionary AI call-deflection system designed to waste scammers' time and thwart their malicious efforts.
Nearly a decade ago, a telemarketer crossed the line, swearing at Anderson's son. Fueled by frustration and a desire for revenge, he founded the Jolly Roger Telephone Company and unleashed his ingenious creation: a ChatGPT-powered personality that engages fraudsters in never-ending conversations.
Unwanted robocalls are the top complaint to the Federal Communication Commission, with Americans receiving a staggering four billion such calls each month. These calls result in the loss of billions of dollars annually. But Anderson's AI, powered by ChatGPT, has proven to be a formidable adversary.
With the ability to adapt its responses to the context of the call, the AI keeps scammers entangled, teasing them about debt consolidation or discussing Medicare fraud. Anderson's AI can even portray various personalities, from an elderly man with memory loss to a distracted mother or sports enthusiast.
Preventing robocall scams not only saves Americans billions of dollars but also restores trust in answering the phone. Anderson hopes his AI tool provides everyone a sense of revenge and protection against these nuisances.
Through the power of artificial intelligence, Anderson's solution presents a glimmer of hope in the ongoing battle against scammers and unwanted robocalls. With determination and innovation, he shows us that sometimes the most "incredibly silly ideas" can pave the way towards a brighter future for humanity.
