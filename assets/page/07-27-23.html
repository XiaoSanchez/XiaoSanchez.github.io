Casetify's Evangelion iPhone and AirPods Cases: A Stylish Defense

Project information

  • Category: App
  • Project date: July 27, 2023

Casetify's new Evangelion-inspired line offers iPhone and AirPods cases designed to shield against impacts.
Collaborating with Studio Khara, the cases wrap devices in protective layers, minimizing damage from falls.
The collection includes cases featuring Evangelion pilots, MagSafe cases resembling mechs, a charging dock inspired by ejection stands, and an AirPods Pro case shaped like Eva Test Type-01's head.
To mark the debut on July 20th, Casetify hosts an outdoor exhibition in Japan, showcasing a 13-foot EVA-01 until July 25th.
While pricing is undisclosed, fans can join a waitlist and receive AR marker cards unlocking digital product replicas.
An innovative blend of style and protection.
