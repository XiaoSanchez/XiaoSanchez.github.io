Miyazaki's New Film 'How Do You Live?' Coming in IMAX Version!

Project information

  • Category: AI
  • Project date: 12 July, 2023

Fans are eagerly anticipating the release of 'How Do You Live?' - the latest film from renowned Japanese animation director Hayao Miyazaki. After six years in the making, the movie is set to hit Japanese theaters on July 14th, with the added bonus of an IMAX version, a first for Studio Ghibli.
Miyazaki, known for his unique artistry and profound storytelling, last released a feature-length animated film a decade ago. While little is known about the plot, audiences can expect his signature attention to detail and exploration of human nature, environmental issues, and social themes.
The film is based on a novel by Genzaburo Yoshino, where a 15-year-old boy named "Kokei" embarks on a journey guided by his uncle, exploring friendship, wealth disparity, discrimination, courage, and human worth.
Unlike typical marketing strategies, Studio Ghibli's producer, Toshio Suzuki, decided to forgo trailers, posters, and promotional materials, aiming to surprise audiences with a fresh, immersive experience when they directly enter theaters to watch the film.
Overall, the buzz surrounding 'How Do You Live?' is palpable, as fans eagerly await Miyazaki's return and the opportunity to experience his captivating storytelling once again on the big screen.
