A Journey into Distributed Systems

Project information

  • Category: App
  • Project date: August 1, 2023

Building a personal RPC project is a transformative journey into the realm of distributed systems. This project revolves around creating an RPC framework from scratch, providing hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of critical concepts. By implementing fundamental components like serialization, deserialization, and network communication, we gain insights into the intricate workings of distributed systems.
One of the core features we will explore is load balancing, where we distribute requests among server instances efficiently using strategies such as round-robin or least active call count. Additionally, we will delve into automatic restarts and fault tolerance mechanisms, ensuring the system remains resilient in the face of network interruptions or failures.
The project's significance lies in the comprehensive testing framework, allowing us to verify the correctness and reliability of our RPC framework. Through this, we cultivate essential testing skills and the ability to validate complex systems.
A major benefit of building our own RPC project is customization. We have the freedom to tailor the framework to our specific needs, addressing unique requirements that may not be accommodated by existing solutions.
Throughout this endeavor, we also acquire proficiency in network programming, learning to establish connections, manage communication, and overcome potential challenges. The documentation and illustrative examples we provide will assist others in understanding and effectively utilizing our yrpc framework.
In conclusion, embarking on the journey to build a personal RPC project is an enriching experience that equips us with in-depth knowledge, hands-on skills, and a deep appreciation for distributed systems. As we complete this project, we emerge as more competent developers, well-prepared to tackle the complexities of real-world distributed applications.
